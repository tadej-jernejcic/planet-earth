Plastic Pollution in Our Oceans

Every year, millions of tons of plastic end up in the world's oceans, posing a severe threat to marine life and ecosystems. From tiny plankton to large whales, plastic affects aquatic life at every level, often resulting in ingestion, starvation, and entanglement. The persistence of plastic waste due to its non-biodegradable nature calls for urgent global changes in plastic use and disposal.
